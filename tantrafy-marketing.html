About Tantrafy

Tantrafy is a guided audio meditation app focused on sexual wellness. Through tantric meditation practices, Tantrafy helps individuals and couples reconnect with their bodies, deepen intimacy, and treat arousal as a sacred, integral part of life — just like exercise, nutrition, and mental health.

Our guided audio sessions are designed for solo practitioners (men and women) and couples. Each session uses breathwork, body awareness, and tantric meditation techniques to help you slow down, feel more, and build a healthier relationship with your body and sexuality.

Frequently Asked Questions

What is tantric meditation?
Tantric meditation is an ancient practice that uses breathwork, body awareness, and mindful attention to sensation to deepen your connection with your body. Unlike conventional meditation that often focuses on stillness or detachment, tantric meditation embraces the body and its sensations as a path to presence and self-awareness.
Is this app appropriate for beginners?
Yes. Our guided sessions are designed to be accessible to anyone, regardless of meditation experience. Each session provides clear, step-by-step guidance.
Does Tantrafy contain explicit content?
No. Tantrafy is an audio-only guided meditation app. There is no explicit imagery, video, or graphic content. Sessions focus on breathwork, body awareness, and mindful connection.
Who are the sessions for?
We offer sessions designed for three audiences: solo sessions for men, solo sessions for women, and guided sessions for couples to practice together.
Is Tantrafy free?
Tantrafy is currently free to use during our early access period. Pricing may change in the future as we add more content and features.
How do I cancel my pre-order?
You can cancel your pre-order at any time through the App Store or Google Play. No charge is made until the app is released.
